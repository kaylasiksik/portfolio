About Me...

I'm currently a BizOps Specialist at an Austin-based cybersecutity firm and a mother to three adorable fur-babies.

  • Name: Kayla Siksik
  • Age: 22
  • Occupation: BizOps Specialist

I was born outside of Paris, France in 1999 to an American mother and a French father. In 2003, a year after my younger brother was born, we moved to California for two years. We then went back to France, where I attended the Lycee International de Saint-Germain en Laye until the end of primary school. In 2011, we moved to Austin, TX, where I completed middle and high school, graduating from Westwood High School in 2017. From 2017 to 2021, I attended McGill University in Montreal, QC until I graduated with a Bachelors in Psychology and a double minor in Behavioral Science and Philosophy. As far as the fun stuff, I grew up with a profound love for reading, dancing for 14 years, and have always loved art. I also dabbled in gymnastics, figure skating, swimming, tennis, sailing, and more.
